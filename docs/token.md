## Utility

### Staking (Relayer Bond)

Ghost Relayer operators stake at least 10,000 ZHAD0 to participate in the network. Staked tokens are subject to slashing for protocol violations.

### Governance

ZHAD0 holders vote on:
- Protocol parameter updates (threshold, minimum stake, fee splits)
- Treasury allocation
- Ecosystem grants
- Emergency pauses

### Fee Sharing

20% of all relay fees (denominated in ETH) are distributed pro-rata to staked ZHAD0 holders.
